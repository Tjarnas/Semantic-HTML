Top 3 Genres

  1. Metal
  2. Rock
  3. EDM

Metal

Since relatively young age I started to listen to pop-punk, heavily influenced by bands like Sum41, Green Day, Rise Against, Paramore etc etc.
My older brother introduced me to metal - Among the first bands my brother exposed me to were Avenged Sevenfold, Atreyu, Bullet For My Valentine, and Disturbed.
The blistering riffs, thunderous drums, and soaring vocals that emanated from their songs instantly captured my imagination.

I was drawn to the raw emotion, the aggressive energy, and the unapologetic nature of the music.
As I delved deeper into the world of metal, my inspiration expanded to include countless other artists.
I found solace in the dark, melodic symphonies which spoke to my soul and fueled my desire to learn how to play the electric guitar.

Rock

As my love for metal grew, I began to explore related genres that shared a similar spirit of rebellion and passion.
One such genre that soon captured my attention was rock. While metal had initially been my gateway into the world of heavy music, rock brought a refreshing blend of melody and attitude that resonated deeply with me.

While metal will always hold a special place in my heart, rock introduced itself as a dynamic sibling, sharing a lineage of raw energy and powerful emotions.
Together, they have enriched my musical journey, shaping my tastes and influencing my own creative endeavors.
From the intense heaviness of metal to the melodic allure of rock, I continue to be in awe of the transformative power that music possesses.

EDM

While my musical journey had primarily revolved around the realms of metal and rock, fate had an unexpected surprise in store for me.
It was through my love for PC gaming that I found myself inadvertently introduced to the vibrant and pulsating world of EDM, or Electronic Dance Music.

As I explored the expansive world of EDM, I found myself attending music festivals and live performances, witnessing firsthand the electrifying atmosphere that enveloped the crowds.
The pulsating lights, euphoric vibes, and unity among fans created an indescribable sense of euphoria and connection.

Architects

About:

Hailing from Brighton, England, Architects emerged onto the metal scene with an explosive intensity that demanded attention.
Since their formation in 2004, the band has continuously evolved and refined their sound, creating a sonic tapestry that effortlessly blends elements of metalcore, progressive metal, and atmospheric textures.

At the core of Architects' sound lies a masterful combination of aggressive guitar riffs, bone-shaking breakdowns, and intricate melodies.
Their songs possess a dynamic range that sweeps listeners from moments of seething aggression to ethereal beauty, showcasing a musical dexterity that sets them apart.
With each album, they push the boundaries of their own creativity, crafting compositions that are equal parts devastating and transcendent.

OVERWERK

About:

Overwerk, a visionary artist who has carved his own path within the vast landscape of electronic dance music.
With his distinct blend of captivating melodies, pulsating beats, and a unique audiovisual experience, Overwerk has established himself as a true pioneer in the realm of electronic music.

From the moment his music graces your ears, you will be transported into a world of sonic wonder crafted by the mastermind known as Overwerk.
Hailing from Canada, this enigmatic producer and composer has garnered a devoted following with his ability to seamlessly fuse elements of electro, house, and synthwave into a mesmerizing sonic tapestry.

Top 3 Albums

Holy Hell - Architects

About:

Holy Hell delves deep into themes of loss, grief, and the struggle for hope in the face of adversity.
The album serves as a tribute to the band's late guitarist and founding member, Tom Searle, whose tragic passing deeply impacted the entire band and their creative process.
The lyrics are introspective, introspective, and poignant, offering a glimpse into the raw emotions experienced by the band members during this challenging time.

Echoes, Silence, Patience & Grace - Foo Fighters

About:

Echoes, Silence, Patience & Grace is a sonic journey that effortlessly balances the raw energy of rock with introspective introspection and melodic beauty.
Released in 2007, this album showcases the band's musical maturity and their ability to craft songs that resonate deeply with listeners.

Vessel - OVERWERK

About:
